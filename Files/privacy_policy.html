Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how our app collects, uses, and protects your information.

1. Information We Collect

We do not collect any personally identifiable information from users directly without consent.

However, the following third-party services and features may collect or handle information automatically:

  • Google AdMob: Used to display ads. It may collect device info, IP address, and usage statistics.
  • Google Analytics: Helps us understand how users interact with the app.
  • Firebase Cloud Messaging: Used to send push notifications.
  • Google OAuth Login: Allows sign-in using a Google account. We may collect name, email, and profile picture (with consent).
  • Public Chat: Messages are visible to all users. Please avoid sharing sensitive data.
  • Block / Report Feature: Users can block or report other users. Reports are used for moderation only.
  • Admin Ban: Admins may ban users who violate rules. Ban information may be linked to account or device.

2. How We Use Information

  • To display relevant ads (AdMob).
  • To analyze usage and improve app performance (Analytics).
  • To send push notifications (FCM).
  • To authenticate users (Google Login).
  • To manage public chat and handle moderation (Block, Report, Ban).

3. Data Security

We do not store personal data on our servers. All services used follow industry-standard security practices.

4. Children's Privacy

This app is not directed toward children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy at any time. Continued use of the app means you agree to the updated policy.

6. User Data Deletion Policy

Our app does not require registration unless you log in with Google. Data collected via third-party services is managed by them according to their policies.

We do not provide a direct way to delete data from within the app, but Google and Firebase may delete or anonymize data automatically within 90 days.

If you would like to request data deletion related to your login or ban, please contact us at: phyonyinyihtet57@gmail.com

7. Data Collection

  • Device Info: Device ID, OS version, app version (for performance monitoring).
  • Crash Logs: Anonymous crash data (Crashlytics).
  • Usage Info: Screens visited, time spent, etc. (Analytics).
  • Ad Data: Advertising ID for personalized ads (AdMob).
  • Login Info: Name, email, profile picture (Google Login).
  • Chat & Report Info: Chat content (public only), block/report actions stored for moderation.
